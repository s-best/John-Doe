ABOUT ME

I have been exploring and recording landscapes through photography for the past ten years. I got my start when I received a hand-me-down camera from my Grandfather, who inspired me to pursue camera based artistry. After studying for a Bachelors degree I delved into the landscapes around me and began recording my adventures, travelling farther with each trip.
